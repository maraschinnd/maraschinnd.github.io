CNC Plotter
December 2017

This is one of the most exciting projects that I've worked on during my time in school. It is a fully functional CNC plotter prototype with 2.5 axis motion. It can draw on a surface that is approximately 11 inches by 11 inches and has the ability to lift and drop the writing utensil. The entire thing is controlled by an Arduino utilizing a CNC shield, a combination of stepper motors, lead screws, linear rails and bearings, and a servo motor for the Z axis. I have created a seperate website detailing this entire project. Check it out! Go Plot Yourself Website
